The Sil-Q application icon in this repository is a cropped detail of a painting
by John Howe depicting the duel between Fingolfin and Morgoth. The same
painting appears on the cover of Sil-Q's own game manual, and the icon is
reused here, in that same spirit, solely to identify the packaged game.

The painting is the copyrighted work of John Howe. It is not covered by any of
the free licenses used elsewhere in this repository, and no license to the
artwork is claimed or granted.

If the copyright holder does not wish this artwork to be published here, it
will be removed on request.
